About

Engineer, Designer, Maker, Developer

I'm Luis and I like making things, mostly for the sake of learning about data analysis, signal processing, and crafting useful stuff. Sometimes I start small electronics projects, armed with a soldering iron, some microcontrollers and sensors I have lying around catching dust, an FDM 3D printer, and of course, a lot of time reading forums, Reddit, articles, books, and blogs from other makers. Equally important: months of YouTube videos. I never said I finish the projects though, but I plan to document the ones I did finish and force myself to complete the others. I also plan on sharing some of the stuff I learn along my journey.

I'm Venezuelan but have been living in Turin, Italy for the past six years. When I'm not making stuff, I am probably hanging out with my girlfriend, Uri, having drinks and food with some friends, or out for a hike or a short bicycle trip.

Uri and I actually started sharing some of the stuff we have made together, with a more artistic (?) side. You may find more interesting stuff there as well.
